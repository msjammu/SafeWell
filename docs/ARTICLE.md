## The moment that started this

On a June morning in 2026, a 4-year-old named **Nirvair Singh** walked across a field in
Dhanaura village, Ambala, while his father took breakfast to his grandfather. Playing near
the edge, he slipped into a neighbour's **uncovered, 350-foot borewell**. He lodged around
220 feet down — and then slipped deeper as rescuers worked. NDRF, SDRF, the Army, cameras,
hooks, and heavy machinery were deployed. Rain poured in. His grandfather refused to leave
the spot.

The District Commissioner's verdict was blunt: the borewell **should have been covered**.

If you've followed Indian news for any length of time, you know this story — because it keeps
happening.

## This is not a rare accident

It's a recurring, preventable failure:

- **From Prince (2006) to today.** The 2006 televised rescue of *Prince* in Haryana gripped
  the nation. Nearly two decades later — through **Sujith Wilson** (Tamil Nadu, 2019),
  **Chetna Choudhary** (Rajasthan, 2024), and dozens more — little has structurally changed.
- **~40 child borewell deaths were recorded between 2009 and 2019 alone**, and many go
  unrecorded.
- India has an estimated **27 million borewells**. When one dries up, the cap and casing are
  often pulled out, leaving a narrow, deep, invisible trap.
- **Rescue frequently falls short — even after the child is reached.** Outcomes are often
  heartbreaking, due to slipping deeper, oxygen depletion, water ingress, hypothermia, and
  injuries.

> The hardest, most important truth: **by the time a rescue starts, it's often already too
> late.** So the highest-leverage place to act is *before* the fall.

## What SafeWell is

**SafeWell** is an open project to prevent borewell tragedies across three pillars:

1. **Prevent** — a geo-tagged **registry** of borewells + a **public "report an open well"**
   app + **owner accountability and compliance** tracking. This directly targets the
   enforcement gap that every news report identifies.
2. **Detect** — a low-cost **IoT smart-cap** that senses an uncovered well or a fall and
   **alerts authorities in real time** (later phase).
3. **Respond** — a **rescue-coordination dashboard** with live telemetry to cut the
   detection-to-dispatch lag (later phase).

We're starting with **Phase 1 (Prevent)** because it's fully buildable in software, needs no
hardware to deliver value, and tackles the root cause.

- 🏆 **A hackathon project.** SafeWell is being shaped into a hackathon build — a focused MVP

- 🌍 **Open for public participation.** This isn't a closed effort. The problem is too big and
  too important. We want builders, domain experts, and affected communities at the table.
